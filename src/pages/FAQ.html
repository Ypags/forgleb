  • How do I care for my watch?

    Care for it as a piece of jewelry. Wear it gently and store it in a cool dry environment. It may need occasional polishing to avoid tarnish/oxidation. This can be done with a silver cloth or by applying a small amount of silver polishing compound and buffing it off with a soft microfibre cloth.

  • Is my watch water resistant?

    Yes, each watch is water resistant. They are not designed to be exposed to or submerged in water, especially not at depth.
